This is the place to be

Ghent | Flanders | Belgium

Zuiderpoort is situated in the city of Ghent, more specifically part of Ledeberg which is one of the many districts in the city. The building has a strategic location, close to the highway, the center, train stations of Gent Sint-Pieters and Gent-Dampoort, bus stops of De Lijn as well as a FREE bus shuttle from the main station. Therefore it's easily accesible by car, public transport, bicycle and even on foot from one of the train stations.

  • By bicycle:

    • Gent Sint-Pieters station: 9 minutes

    • Gent Dampoort station: 10 minutes

  • On foot:

    • Gent Sint-Pieters station: 27 minutes

    • Gent Dampoort station: 35 minutes
